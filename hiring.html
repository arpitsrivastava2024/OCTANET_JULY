Our Story

Founded in 1996 by Mr.VSS Mani, MD & CEO, Just Dial started off by offering local search information services and rose to become India’s No.1 player in the category. Over the years, Just Dial has been transitioning from being purely a provider of local search and related information to being an enabler of such transactions. Reliance Retail Ventures Limited acquired sole control of Just Dial from September 1, 2021.

First Interview
During the first round of interviews, one of our recruiters will be conducting the session. They will delve into the details of the role and your relevant experience. This presents an excellent chance for you to inquire about any aspects of the position and also to request any accommodations you may need.
03
Second Interview
The upcoming interview will be conducted with the Business hiring manager. Its primary aim is to evaluate both your technical and soft skills. Additionally, it presents an opportunity to gain further insights into Justdial and the specific team you could potentially become a part of. Depending on the role, there might be subsequent interviews or a technical task to gauge your skill level. For further information on this matter, your recruiter will furnish you with additional details as needed.
04
Feedback and Offer
Congratulations on successfully completing the interview stages! At this point, your recruiter will gather feedback from the interviewers and share it with you.
